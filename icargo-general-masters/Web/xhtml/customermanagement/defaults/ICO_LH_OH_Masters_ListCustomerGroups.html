Masters

LIST CUSTOMER GROUPS

(Screen ID – CMT003)

 

Click here to view screen access information.

This screen is accessed from the iCargo menu.

Airline Functions > Masters > Customer Group > List Customer Groups

 

The Customer Management department and users with adequate privileges can use the List Customer Groups screen.

Functions

View list of customer groups

Attach/Detach loyalty programmes to/from a customer group

Delete a customer group from the system

Create a new customer group

Locate a customer group and view/modify its details

 

View list of customer groups

 

1.      Open the List Customer Groups screen ( Airline Functions > Masters > Customer Group > List Customer Groups ).

 

2.      In the List Customer Groups screen, specify the required search criteria, none are mandatory.

 

3.      Click List. (mandatory)

The list of customer groups that match the specified search criteria appears in thetable.

NOTE:

·    To view all the customer groups , click List without specifying any search criteria.

·    To clear all entries simultaneously, click Clear.

 

4.      To exit the screen, click Close.

Field/Button Description – List Customer Groups screen

 

Attach/Detach loyalty programmes to/from a customer group

 

1.      Open the List Customer Groups screen ( Airline Functions > Masters > Customer Group > List Customer Groups ).

 

2.      In the List Customer Groups screen, list the customer groups. (mandatory)

 

3.      In the table, select the required customer group from the table section. (mandatory)

 

4.      Click Attach/Detach. (mandatory)

The Attach Group Code To Customers screen appears. The list of loyalty programmes, if any, already attached to this customer group are displayed.

Field/Button Description – Attach Group Code To Customers screen

 

5.      In the Attach Group Code To Customers screen:

·          To attach a loyalty programme to a customer group:

a)Click the Add link.

      A new row appears in the table.

b)Type or select the following:

·          Loyalty Programme

·          Attach From Date

·          Attach To Date

 

·          To detach an existing loyalty programme from a customer group:

a)     In the table, select the loyalty programme.

b)     Click the Delete link.

 

6.      Click Save. (mandatory)

The loyalty programme is attached / detached and the List Customer Groups screen reappears.

 

7.      To exit the screen, click Close.

Field/Button Description – List Customer Groups screen

 

Delete a customer group

 

1.      Open the List Customer Groups screen ( Airline Functions > Masters > Customer Group > List Customer Groups ).

 

2.      In the List Customer Groups screen, list the customer groups. (mandatory)

 

3.      In the table, select the customer groups to be deleted.

 

4.      Click Delete. Click Yes in the confirmation message that appears. (mandatory)

The selected customer groups are removed from the system.

 

5.      To exit the screen, click Close.

Field/Button Description – List Customer Groups screen

 

Create a new customer group

 

1.      Open the List Customer Groups screen ( Airline Functions > Masters > Customer Group > List Customer Groups ).

 

2.      Click Create. (mandatory)

The Maintain Customer Group screen appears.

Field/Button Description –Maintain Customer Group screen

 

3.      In the Maintain Customer Group screen:

a)     Capture the group details.

b)     Click Save.

The group details are saved and the List Customer Groups screen reappears.

 

4.      To exit the screen, click Close.

Field/Button Description – List Customer Groups screen

 

Locate a customer group and view/modify its details

 

1.      Open the List Customer Groups screen ( Airline Functions > Masters > Customer Group > List Customer Groups ).

 

2.      In the List Customer Groups screen, list the customer groups. (mandatory)

 

3.      In the table, select the customer groups to be viewed or modified.

 

4.      Click Details. (mandatory)

The Maintain Customer Group screen appears with the details of the selected customer group.

Field/Button Description –Maintain Customer Group screen

 

5.      In the Maintain Customer Group screen:

a)     Make the required changes.

b)     Click Save.

The modified group details are saved and the List Customer Groups screen reappears.

 

6.      To exit the screen, click Close.

Field/Button Description – List Customer Groups screen
