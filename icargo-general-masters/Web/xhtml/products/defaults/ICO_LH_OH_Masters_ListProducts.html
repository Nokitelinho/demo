List Products

(Screen ID – PDS002)

 

Click here to view screen access information.

This screen is accessed from the iCargo menu.

Airline Functions > Masters > Products and Services > List Products .

 

When you access the List Products screen, default values are displayed in the following fields:

·        Status – ‘ALL’

·        Transport Mode – ‘ALL’

·        Priority – ‘ALL’

 

The Airline Product Manager can use the List Products screen.

Note:

·        To create a new product, click Create in List Products screen. This takes you to the Maintain Products screen, where you can create a new product.

 

·        To view/modify product details:

-   If you are not aware of the Product Code, you can use the List Products screen. In this screen, you can provide the search criteria, locate the required product and then view/modify its details.

 

-   However, if you know the Product Code, you can use the Maintain Products screen. Specify the Product Code, and then display its details for viewing/modification.

View list of products

 

1.      Open the List Productsscreen ( Airline Functions > Masters > Products and Services > List Products).

 

2.      In the List Products screen, specify the search criteria in the Search Criteria section. None are mandatory.

 

3.      Click List. (mandatory)

The list of the products that satisfy the specified search criteria appear in the Product Details table.

Note:

To view the list of products in the system, click List without specifying any search criteria.

 

4.      To exit the screen, click Close.

Field/Button Description – List Products screen

 

Locate a product and view/modify its details

If you are not aware of the Product Code, you can use the List Products screen. In this screen, you can specify the search criteria, locate the required product and then view/modify its details.

 

Note:

However, if you know the Product Code, you can use the Maintain Products screen. Specify the Product Code, and then display its details for viewing/modification.

 

1.      Open the List Productsscreen ( Airline Functions > Masters > Products and Services > List Products).

 

2.      In the List Products screen, list the products. (mandatory)

 

3.      In the Product Details table, select the required row. (mandatory)

 

4.      Click Details. (mandatory)

The Maintain Products screen appears with the details of the selected product.

View details of a product

 

5.      In the Maintain Products screen:

a)     Make the required changes.

Note:

To discontinue the process and return to the List Products screen, click Close.

Modify details of a product

b)     Click Save. (mandatory)

The modifications are saved.

c)      Click Close. (mandatory)

The List Products screen appears with the updated details of the product.

Field/Button Description – Maintain Products screen

 

6.      To exit the screen, click Close.

View list of sub products associated with a product

 

1.      Open the List Productsscreen ( Airline Functions > Masters > Products and Services > List Products).

 

2.      In the List Products screen, list the products. (mandatory)

 

3.      In the Product Details table, select the required row for which the sub products are to be listed. (mandatory)

 

4.      Click List Sub Products. (mandatory)

The Maintain Products screen appears with the list of sub products of the selected product.

Note:

To exit the screen and return to List Products screen, click Close.

Save an existing product as a new product

 

1.      Open the List Productsscreen ( Airline Functions > Masters > Products and Services > List Products).

 

2.      In the List Products screen, list the products. (mandatory)

 

3.      In the Product Details table, select the required row. (mandatory)

 

4.      Click Save as New Product . (mandatory)

The Maintain Products screen appears. This screen displays the details of the product.

 

5.      In the Maintain Products screen:

a)    Make the required changes.

b)    Click Save. (mandatory)

The changes are saved for the new product.

c)    Click Close. (mandatory)

Field/Button Description – List Products screen

 

Activate a product

 

1.      Open the List Productsscreen ( Airline Functions > Masters > Products and Services > List Products).

 

2.      In the List Products screen, list the products. (mandatory)

 

3.      In the Product Details table, select the required row to be activated. (mandatory)

Note:

You can activate a product only if rate is defined for the selected product.

 

4.      Click Activate. (mandatory)

Click Yes in the confirmation message that appears. The selected product is activated.

 

5.      To exit the screen, click Close.

Field/Button Description – List Products screen

 

Inactivate a product

 

1.      Open the List Productsscreen ( Airline Functions > Masters > Products and Services > List Products).

 

2.      In the List Products screen, list the products. (mandatory)

 

3.      In the Product Details table, select the required row to be inactivated. (mandatory)

Note:

You can inactivate only those products which have the Status as ‘Active’.

 

4.      Click Inactivate. (mandatory)

Click Yes in the confirmation message that appears. The selected product is inactivated.

 

5.      To exit the screen, click Close.

Field/Button Description – List Products screen

 

Locate a product and activate its sub product

 

1.      Open the List Productsscreen ( Airline Functions > Masters > Products and Services > List Products).

 

2.      In the List Products screen, list the products. (mandatory)

 

3.      In the Product Details table, select the required row and click List Sub Products. (mandatory)

The Maintain Products screen appears with the sub products associated with the selected product.

 

4.      In the Maintain Products screen:

a)    In the Sub Product Details table, select the row to be activated. (mandatory)

Note:

Only sub products with Status as ‘Inactive’ can be activated.

b)    Click Activate. (mandatory)

Click Yes in the confirmation message that appears. The selected sub product is activated.

c)    Click Close. (mandatory)

The List Products screen reappears.

 

5.      To exit the screen, click Close.

Field/Button Description – List Products screen

Field/Button Description – List Products screen

 

Locate a product and view/modify its sub product

 

1.      Open the List Productsscreen ( Airline Functions > Masters > Products and Services > List Products).

 

2.      In the List Products screen, list the products. (mandatory)

 

3.      In the Product Details table, select the required row and click List Sub Products. (mandatory)

The Maintain Products screen appears with the sub products associated with the selected product.

 

4.      In the Maintain Products screen:

a)    In the Sub Product Details table, select the row whose details are to be modified. (mandatory)

b)    Click Details. (mandatory)

A screen appears with the details of the sub product.

c)    In this screen:

i)     Make the required changes, if any. The following cannot be edited:

·        Product Name

·        Status

·        Start Date

·        End Date

·        Transport Mode

·        SCC

·        Priority

·        Services

All other details in the screen can be edited.

Note:

The time specifications for milestones and the restrictions specified in tis screen overrides those specified for the product.

ii)    Click Save. (mandatory)

Click Yes in the confirmation message that appears. The modifications are saved.

iii)   Click Close. (mandatory)

The Maintain Products screen reappears.

d)    Click Close. (mandatory)

The List Products screen reappears .

 

5.      To exit the screen, click Close.

Field/Button Description – List Products screen

 

Delete a product from the system

 

1.      Open the List Productsscreen ( Airline Functions > Masters > Products and Services > List Products).

 

2.      In the List Products screen, list the products. (mandatory)

 

3.      In the Product Details table, select the required row. (mandatory)

 

4.      Click Delete. (mandatory)

Click Yes in the confirmation message that appears. The selected product is deleted from the system.

 

5.      To exit the screen, click Close.

Product Name

Status

drop-down list

The status of the product.

Possible values:

·   ALL

·   Active

·   Inactive

·   New

 

·   By default the option ‘ALL’ is selected.

 

·   Select this from the drop-down list.

O

Rate Defined

check box

If the check box is selected, it indicates that the rate is defined for product.

 

Select the check box if required.

 

O

Transport Mode

drop-down list

The mode of transportation(s) available for the product.

Possible values:

·   A2A – Airport to Airport

·   A2D – Airport to Door

·   D2A – Door to Airport

·   D2D – Door to Door

·   ALL

 

·   By default the option ‘ALL’ is selected.

 

·   Select this from the drop-down list.

 

·   To open the list, click the arrow in the field.

 

O

Priority

drop-down list

The priority for the product.

Possible values:

·   ALL

·   High

·   Low

·   Medium

 

·   By default the option ‘ALL’ is selected.

 

·   Select this from the drop-down list.

 

·   To open the list, click the arrow in the field.

 

O

SCC

·   Type this in the field or select this from the LOV.

 

·   To open the LOV, click icon adjacent to the field.
