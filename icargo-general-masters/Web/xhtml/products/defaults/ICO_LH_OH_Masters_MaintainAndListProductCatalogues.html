MASTERS

MAINTAIN AND LIST PRODUCT CATALOGUE

(Screen ID – PDS004)

 

Click here to view screen access information.

This screen is accessed from the iCargo menu.

Airline Functions > Masters > Products and Services > Maintain and List Product Catalogues


When you access the screen, by default, the list of all the products is displayed.


The Airline Product Manager can use the Maintain and List Product Catalogues screen.

Functions

View a product brochure

Print a product brochure

View the catalogue of a product

Produce feedback about a product

Check station availability of a product

Send email about a product

Print a product catalogue

 

View a product brochure

 

1.      Open the Maintain and List Product Catalogues screen. ( Airline Functions > Masters > Products and Services > Maintain and List Product Catalogues)

 

2.      In the Product Details table select the row corresponding to the product whose brochure is to be viewed. (mandatory)

 

3.      Click View Product Brochure. (mandatory)

The product brochure appears as a report.

 

4.      To exit the screen, click Close.

Field/Button Description – Maintain and List Product Catalogue screen

 

Print a product brochure

 

1.      Open the Maintain and List Product Catalogues screen. ( Airline Functions > Masters > Products and Services > Maintain and List Product Catalogues)

 

2.      In the Product Details table select the row corresponding to the product whose brochure to be printed .(mandatory)

 

3.      Click Print Product Brochure. (mandatory)

The product brochure gets printed.

 

4.      To exit the screen, click Close.

Field/Button Description – Maintain and List Product Catalogue screen

 

View the catalogue of a product

 

1.      Open the Maintain and List Product Catalogues screen. ( Airline Functions > Masters > Products and Services > Maintain and List Product Catalogues)

 

2.      In the Product Details table select the row corresponding to the product whose catalogue is to be viewed.( mandatory)

 

3.      Click View. (mandatory)

The ProductCatalogue screen appears with the details of the product.

Note:

To return to the Maintain and List Product Catalogues screen, click Close.

Field/Button Description – Product Catalogue screen

 

4.      To exit the screen, click Close.

Field/Button Description – Maintain and List Product Catalogue screen

 

Produce feedback about a product

 

1.      Open the Maintain and List Product Catalogues screen. ( Airline Functions > Masters > Products and Services > Maintain and List Product Catalogues)

 

2.      In the Product Details table select the row corresponding to the product whose details to be viewed .(mandatory)

 

3.      Click View. (mandatory)

The ProductCatalogue screen appears with the details of the product.

Field/Button Description – Product Catalogue screen

 

4.      In the ProductCatalogue screen:

a)    Click Feedback .(mandatory)

The Feedback screen appears.

Field/Button Description – Feedback screen

i)     In the Feedback screen, specify the details. The following fields are mandatory:

-   Type Name

-   Type Email

ii)    Click Send . (mandatory)

The feedback about the product is send to the corresponding airline. Click OK in the dialog box that appears. The ProductCatalogue screen reappears.

iii)   Click Close. (mandatory)

The Maintain and List Product Catalogues screen reappears.

5.      To exit the screen, click Close.

Field/Button Description – Maintain and List Product Catalogue screen

 

Check station availability of a product

 

1.      Open the Maintain and List Product Catalogues screen. ( Airline Functions > Masters > Products and Services > Maintain and List Product Catalogues)

 

2.      In the Product Details table select the row corresponding to the product whose details to be viewed. (mandatory)

 

3.      Click View. (mandatory)

The ProductCatalogue screen appears with the details of the product.

Field/Button Description – Product Catalogue screen

 

4.      In the ProductCatalogue screen:

a)    Click Check Station Availability. (mandatory)

The Check Availability screen appears.

Field/Button Description – Check Availability screen

i)     In the Check Availability screen , specify the details. The following fields are mandatory:

-   Type or select Origin

-   Type or select Destination

-   Type or select Commodity

ii)    Click Check. (mandatory)

The availability of the product is checked for the specified segment and commodity.

iv)   Click Close. (mandatory)

The ProductCatalogue screen reappears.

 

5.      In the ProductCatalogue screen, click Close.

The Maintain and List Product Catalogues screen reappears.

 

6.      To exit the screen, click Close.

Field/Button Description – Maintain and List Product Catalogue screen

 

Send email about a product

1.      Open the Maintain and List Product Catalogues screen. ( Airline Functions > Masters > Products and Services > Maintain and List Product Catalogues)

 

2.      In the Product Details table select the row corresponding to the product whose details to be viewed. (mandatory)

 

3.      Click View. (mandatory)

Field/Button Description – Product Catalogue screen

 

4.      In the ProductCatalogue screen:

a)    Click Email. (mandatory)

The Send Email screen appears.

Field/Button Description – Send Email screen

i)     In the Send Email screen , specify the details. The following fields are mandatory:

-   Type Your Name

-   Type Your Email

-   Type Friend’s Name

-   Type Friend’s Email

ii)    Click Send . (mandatory)

The feedback about the product is send to the friend.

iii)   Click Close. (mandatory)

The ProductCatalogue screen reappears.

 

5.      In the ProductCatalogue screen, click Close.

The Maintain and List Product Cataloguesscreen reappears.

 

6.      To exit the screen, click Close.

Field/Button Description – Maintain and List Product Catalogue screen
