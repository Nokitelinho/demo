Damage Checklist Master

(Screen ID – ULD040)

Click here to view screen access information.

To access the Damage Checklist Master screen:
Select the ULD Management tab. On the ULD Maintenance menu, click Damage Checklist Master.

 

The ULD Controller can use the Damage Checklist Master screen.

Create damage checklist for a ULD

 

1.      Select the ULD Management tab. On the ULD Maintenance menu, click Damage Checklist Master to get the Damage Checklist Master screen.

 

2.      In the Damage Checklist Master screen, click List. (mandatory)

All the links and buttons become enabled.

 

3.      Click the Add link. (mandatory)

A new row appears in the table.

 

4.      In the table, specify the following mandatory details:

·        Select the Section.

·        Type the Description.

·        Type the No. Of Points.

 

5.      You can add more damage checklists for ULD. To do this, click the Add link and repeat the previous step. (optional)

 

6.      Click Save. (mandatory)

The damage checklist details are saved.

 

7.      To exit the screen, click Close.

Field/Button Description – Damage Checklist Master screen

View list of damage checklists

 

1.      Select the ULD Management tab. On the ULD Maintenance menu, click Damage Checklist Master to get the Damage Checklist Master screen.

 

2.   In the Damage Checklist Master screen, select the Section. (optional)

 

3.   Click List. (mandatory)

The list of damage checklists corresponding to the selected ULD section appears in the table.

 

Note:

To view all damage checklists captured in the system, click List without specifying any search criteria.

 

4.   To exit the screen, click Close.

Field/Button Description – Damage Checklist Master screen

 

Modify damage checklist for a ULD

 

1.      Select the ULD Management tab. On the ULD Maintenance menu, click Damage Checklist Master to get the Damage Checklist Master screen.

 

2.      In the Damage Checklist Master screen, list the damage checklists. (mandatory)

 

3.      In the table, modify the damage checklist details in the required row.

Section is not editable.

Note:

You can modify multiple damage checklist details at a time. To do this, make the required changes in all corresponding rows.

 

4.      Click Save. (mandatory)

The modifications are saved.

 

5.      To exit the screen, click Close.

Field/Button Description – Damage Checklist Master screen

 

Delete damage checklist from the system

 

1.      Select the ULD Management tab. On the ULD Maintenance menu, click Damage Checklist Master to get the Damage Checklist Master screen.

 

2.      In the Damage Checklist Master screen, list the damage checklists. (mandatory)

 

3.      In the table, select the row to be deleted. (mandatory)

Note:

You can delete multiple damage checklists at a time. To do this, select all the corresponding rows.

 

4.      Click the Delete link. (mandatory)

The selected damage checklist details are deleted from the table.

 

5.      Click Save. (mandatory)

The selected damage checklist details are deleted from the system.

 

6.      To exit the screen, click Close.
