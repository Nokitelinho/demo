The Segment Exception screen is accessed from the Pool Ownership Master screen.
To access it:

- ULD Management > Stock Management > ULD Pool Owners to get the Pool Ownership Master screen.
- In Pool Ownership Master screen list the pool ownerships.
- In the Pool Ownership Details section, select a row and click Segment Exceptions.
