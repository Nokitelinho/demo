list damage reports

(Screen ID – ULD003)

Click here to view screen access information.

To access the List Damage Reports screen:
Select the ULD Management tab. On the ULD Maintenance menu, click List Damage Reports.

RELATED TOPICS

When you access the screen, default values are displayed in the following fields:

·        ULD Status. – ‘ALL’

·        Damage Status - ‘ALL’

·        Current Airport – The logged in airport

·        Party Type – ‘ALL’

 

The screen provides links to Maintain Damage Report and Maintain ULD screens.

 

The ULD Controller can use the List Damage Reports screen.

Note:

To view/modify damage reports:

·        If you are not aware of the ULD Number, you can use the List Damage Reports screen. In this screen, provide your search criteria, locate the required ULD Number and then view/modify the damage report details.

·        However if you know the ULD Number, you can use the Maintain Damage Report screen. Specify the ULD Number and then display its details for viewing/modification.

View list of damage reports

 

1.      Select the ULD Management tab. On the ULD Maintenance menu, click List Damage Reports to get the List Damage Reports screen.

 

2.      In the List Damage Reports screen, specify the search criteria. None are mandatory.

 

3.      Click List. (mandatory)

The lists of damage reports, that match the specified search criteria, appear in the table.

 

Note:

To view all the damage reports, click List without specifying any search criteria.

 

4.      To exit the screen, click Close.

Field/Button Description – List Damage Reports screen

 

Locate a damage report and view/modify its details

If you are not aware of the ULD Number, you can use the List Damage Reports screen to search for, locate and then view/modify details of the required damage report.

 

Note:

However if you know the ULD Number, you can use the Maintain Damage Report screen. In this screen, specify the ULD Number and then display ULD details for viewing/modification.

View the damage details of a ULD

Modify the damage details of a ULD

 

1.      Select the ULD Management tab. On the ULD Maintenance menu, click List Damage Reports to get the List Damage Reports screen.

 

2.      In the List Damage Reports screen, list the damage reports. (mandatory)

 

3.      In the table, select the damage report whose damage details you wish to modify. (mandatory)

 

4.      Click View Damage. (mandatory)

The Maintain Damage Report screen appears.

 

5.      In the Maintain Damage Report screen,

a)    Make the required changes.

Note:

The following fields are non-editable:

-        ULD Number

-        Repair Status

-        Supervisor

-        Total Amount

-        Investigation Report

The following fields are mandatory:

-        Damage Status

-        Overall Status

-        Remarks

Modify the damage  details

b)    Click Save.

The modifications are saved.

c)    Click Close.

The List Damage Reports screen reappears.

Field/Button Description – Maintain Damage Report screen

 

6.      To exit the screen, click Close.

Field/Button Description – List Damage Reports screen

 

Print list of damage reports

 

1.      Select the ULD Management tab. On the ULD Maintenance menu, click List Damage Reports to get the List Damage Reports screen.

 

2.      In the List Damage Reports screen, list the damage reports. (mandatory)

 

3.      Click Print. (mandatory)

The list of damage reports listed on the screen is printed.

 

4.      To exit the screen, click Close.

Field/Button Description – List Damage Reports screen

 

Locate a ULD and view/modify its details

 

1.      Select the ULD Management tab. On the ULD Maintenance menu, click List Damage Reports to get the List Damage Reports screen.

 

2.      In the List Damage Reports screen, list the damage reports. (mandatory)

 

3.      In the table, select the ULD whose details you wish to view/modify.

 

4.      Click ULD Details.

The Maintain ULD screen appears with details of the selected ULD Number.

 

5.      In the Maintain ULD screen,

a)    Make the required changes. The following are non-editable:

-        ULD Number

-        Create Multiple

-        ULD Type Code

-        Airline Code

-        Total No. of ULDs

-        Location

-        Current Airport

Modify the details of a ULD

b)    Click Save.

The modifications are saved .

c)    Click Close.

The List Damage Reports screen reappears.

Field/Button Description – Maintain ULD screen

 

6.      To exit the screen, click Close.

Field/Button Description – List Damage Reports screen

 

Delete a damage report

 

1.      Select the ULD Management tab. On the ULD Maintenance menu, click List Damage Reports to get the List Damage Reports screen.

 

2.      In the List Damage Reports screen, list the damage reports. (mandatory)

 

3.      In the table , select the damage report you wish to delete .(mandatory)

 

Note :

The damage reports with the Status as Open can only be deleted.

 

4.      Click Delete. (mandatory)

Click Yes in the confirmation message that appears.

The selected damage report is deleted from the system.

 

5.      To exit the screen, click Close.
