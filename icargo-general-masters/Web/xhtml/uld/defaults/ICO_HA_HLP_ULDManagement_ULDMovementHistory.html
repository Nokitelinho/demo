ULD MANAGEMENT

ULD MOVEMENT HISTORY

(Screen ID – ULD016)

Click here to view screen access information.

To access the ULD Movement History screen:
Select the ULD Management tab, On the ULD Tracking menu click ULD Movement History.

 

The screen provides a link to the Record ULD Movement screen.

 

The ULD controller can use the ULD History screen .

Functions

List history details of a ULD

Record ULD movement

Generate report on ULD history

 

List history details of a ULD

 

1.      Select the ULD Management tab. On the ULD Tracking menu, click ULD Movement History to get the ULD Movement History screen.

 

2.      In the ULD Movement History screen, type the ULD No. (mandatory)

 

3.      Click List. (mandatory)

The details of the ULD are displayed in the table section.

 

4.      To exit the screen, click Close.

Field/Button Description – ULD Movement History screen

 

Record ULD movement

 

1.      Select the ULD Management tab. On the ULD Tracking menu, click ULD Movement History to get the ULD Movement History screen.

 

2.      In the ULD Movement History screen, list the ULD history details. (mandatory)

 

3.      Click Rec. ULD Movement. (mandatory)

The Record ULD Movement screen appears.

 

4.      In the Record ULD Movement screen.

a)     Make the required changes.

b)     Click Save.

The details are saved and the ULD Movement History reappears.

Field/Button Description – Record ULD Movement screen

 

5.      To exit the screen, click Close.

Field/Button Description – ULD Movement History screen

 

Generate report on ULD history

 

1.      Select the ULD Management tab. On the ULD Tracking menu, click ULD Movement History to get the ULD Movement History screen.

 

2.      In the ULD Movement History screen, list the ULD history details. (mandatory)

 

3.      Click Generate Report. (mandatory)

A report on the history of the ULD is generated.

 

4.      To exit the screen, click Close.
