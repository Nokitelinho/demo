MaINTAIN Damage Report

(Screen ID – ULD012)

Click here to view screen access information.

To access the Maintain Damage Report screen:
Select the ULD Management tab. On the ULD Maintenance menu, click Maintain Damage Report.

 

When you access the screen, default values are displayed in the following fields:

·        Damage Status – ‘Damaged’

·        Overall Status – ‘Non Operational’

·        Repair Status – ‘Repaired’

 

The ULD Controller can use the Maintain Damage Report screen.

Capture the damage details of a ULD

 

1.      Select the ULD Management tab. On the ULD Maintenance menu, click Maintain Damage Report to get the Maintain Damage Report screen.

 

2.      In the Maintain Damage Report screen, type the ULD Number. (mandatory)

 

3.      Click List. (mandatory)

·        If a damaged ULD with the specified number exists in the system, its damage details will be displayed.

·        If damage details of the ULD have not been captured, damage details table will be blank and you can add details to the new ULD number.

 

4.      In the Damage Details section, select the Damage Status and the Overall Status from the respective drop-down lists.

 

5.      You can upload a picture of the damaged ULD, if required. For this, click Browse, locate and upload the required image. (optional)

 

6.      In the Damage Details section, click the Add link. (mandatory)

The Add Damage Details screen appears.

 

7.      In the Add Damage Details screen:

a)   Specify the required details. The following are mandatory.

·        Select the Damage Code.

·        Select the Position.

·        Select the Severity.

·        Type or select the Reported Airport.

 

Note:

·        You can capture multiple damages against same ULD. To do this click the Create link and repeat the above steps.

·        You can delete repair details captured against a ULD. To do this select the required row and click the Delete link.

·        To discontinue the process and return to Maintain Damage Report screen, click Close.

b)   Click OK. (mandatory)

The Maintain Damage Report screen reappears with the captured damage details.

 

8.      You can capture multiple damages against same ULD. To do this click the Add link and repeat the above steps.

 

9.      Click Save. (mandatory)

The damage details are saved.

 

10.   To exit the screen, click Close.

Field/Button Description – Maintain Damage Report screen

 

Capture the repair details of a ULD

 

1.      Select the ULD Management tab. On the ULD Maintenance menu, click Maintain Damage Report to get the Maintain Damage Report screen.

 

2.      In the Maintain Damage Report screen, type the ULD Number. (mandatory)

 

3.      Click List. (mandatory)

If a damaged ULD with the specified number exists in the system, its damage details are be displayed.

Note:

Only for ULDs having damage details, the repair details can be captured.

 

4.      In the Repair Details table, click the Add link. (mandatory)

The Add Repair Details screen appears.

 

5.      In the Add Repair Details screen:

a)   Specify the following mandatory details.

·        Select the Repair Head.

·        Type or select the Repair Airport.

·        Type or select the Repair Date.

·        Select the Damage Ref. No.

·        Type the Amount.

·        Select the Currency.

Note:

To discontinue the process and return to Maintain Damage Report screen, click Close.

b)   Click OK. (mandatory)

The Maintain Damage Report screen reappears with the captured repair details.

Note:

·        You can capture multiple repairs against same ULD. To do this click the Create link and repeat the above steps.

·        You can delete repair details captured against a ULD. To do this select the required row and click the Delete link.

·        To modify repair details of a ULD:

-   Select the required row and click the Modify link.

-   The Add Repair Details screen appears with the repair details.

-   Make the required changes.

Field/Button Description – Add Repair Details screen

 

6.      You can capture multiple repairs against same ULD. To do this click the Add link and repeat the above steps.

 

7.      Click Display Total. (optional)

The total amount for all the repairs done appears in the Total Amount field.

 

8.      Type the Investigation Report. (mandatory)

 

9.      Click Save. (mandatory)

The repair details are saved.

 

10.   To exit the screen, click Close.

Modify the damage details of a ULD

1.      Select the ULD Management tab. On the ULD Maintenance menu, click Maintain Damage Report to get the Maintain Damage Report screen.

 

2.      In the Maintain Damage Report screen, type the ULD Number. (mandatory)

 

3.      Click List. (mandatory)

If a damaged ULD with the specified number exists in the system, its damage details appear in the Damage Details table.

 

4.      In the Damage Details table, select the required row. (mandatory)

 

5.      Click the Modify link. (mandatory)

The Add Damage Details screen appears with the details of the damage.

 

6.      In the Add Damage Details screen:

a)   Make the required changes.

Note:

·        The Damage Ref. No cannot be edited.

·        To discontinue the process and return to Maintain Damage Report screen, click Close.

b)    Click OK. (mandatory)

The Maintain Damage Report screen reappears with the modified damage details.

 

7.      Click Save. (mandatory)

The modifications are saved.

 

8.      To exit the screen, click Close.

Field/Button Description – Maintain Damage Report screen

 

Delete the damage details of a ULD

1.      Select the ULD Management tab. On the ULD Maintenance menu, click Maintain Damage Report to get the Maintain Damage Report screen.

 

2.      In the Maintain Damage Report screen, type the ULD Number. (mandatory)

 

3.      Click List. (mandatory)

If a damaged ULD with the specified number exists in the system, its damage details appear in the Damage Details table.

 

4.      In the Damage Details table, select the required row. (mandatory)

 

5.      Click the Delete link. (mandatory)

The damage details are deleted from the table.

 

6.      Click Save. (mandatory)

The damage details are deleted from the system.

 

7.      To exit the screen, click Close.

Print damage details of a ULD

 

1.      Select the ULD Management tab. On the ULD Maintenance menu, click Maintain Damage Report to get the Maintain Damage Report screen.

 

2.      In the Maintain Damage Report screen, type the ULD Number. (mandatory)

 

3.      Click List. (mandatory)

If a damaged ULD with the specified number exists in the system, its damage details appear in the Damage Details table.

 

4.      Click Print. (mandatory)

The report containing the damage details of the ULD appears in the report viewer. The report can be viewed or printed.

 

5.      To exit the screen, click Close.
